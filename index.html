Wervice ─ Software everywhere




Find free open source applications for business and home. The programs use modern web technologies and common platforms. The applications will continue to be developed. And most times you do not have to install any apps or programs on your laptop or tablet because the apps work with most browsers.






The software and solutions

Wervice Whitespace - Online Desktop Emulator



Wervice Whitespace is an online free desktop emulator.
It works to 100% in the browser. It uses special APIs
to manage files in a storage simple and fast.

Learn more about Whitespace




Interline Word - Text Editor



Write, print and share text everywhere on a tablet, laptop or desktop.
Use Interline Word to write your text easily.
Thanks to a build in storing function, you can save your notes on your device. or Wervice Dashboard.




LockScreenCrypter - Private messages



Simply encrypt your private chat messages.
When the messages appear on the recipient's lock screen
or similar, only strange letters are visible. With LockScreenCrypter, the recipient
can easily decrypt the messages.




Wervice Webuild



Wervice Webuild
