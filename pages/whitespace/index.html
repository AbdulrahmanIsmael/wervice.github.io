Wervice Whitespace

Desktop system on the web

100% free

Wervice Whitespace Trailer


Wervice Whitespace is a free web application to create a desktop-like system in the browser.
Long story, short, ─ Whitespace shows a window environment on every device*.
It works cross-plattform and free in the browser.


Whitespace has lots of helpful feature and pre-installed applications for home and for the work.
Thanks to lots of web apis, web-technologies and free resources, Whitespace runs fast and secure in the browser.
Your Whitespace files and your account are stored local on your computer. In the common browsers, it has up to 5.9 MB storage.**
By using Whitespace, your browser loads Whitespace from the Web and your files and account from your drive.



* The device needs an running OS, connection to the webresource "https://wervice.github.io", connection to the internet and a working web browser.
** This can be different to different devices.
