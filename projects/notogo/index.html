About NoToGo Hide


NoToGo is a free and simple tool to encrypt your messages basically. It makes a bit dificult to read your private messages, without long access to your laptop or phone.
Just copy the decoded message in the encryption window and press encode or copy the encrypted message in the window below and press decode.
Note: This is only a simple encryption. Don't share passwords or something like that using NoToGo.
