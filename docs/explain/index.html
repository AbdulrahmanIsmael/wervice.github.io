 1. No To Go


What does "No To Go" do?

No To Go is an web app. With No To Go, you can encrypt and decrypt private and secret messages. Your recipient has to encrypt the first, so no one can read your messages by having a short view on your recupient's mobile.

Does No To Go store my messages?

No! Only you and your recipient know the message. After refreshing No To Go, your message is removed.

What is Wervice Workspace?

Wervice Workspace is a group of office applications and developers applications for everyone for free. Just like the other Wervice Apps, works Wervice Worksapce in a browser.

About the apps

Interline Word

 3. Wervice WebDesk


WebDesk? What's this?

Wervice WebDesk is an online-working app. With WebDesk you can emulate a desktop system in your browser or on your tablet. Wervice WebDesk has different pre-installed free applications. It stores the data in your browser, so you need no cloud. You can clear your data using WebDesk or by clearing the browser's cache/cookies/history.

How to download files

Sometimes you can download a file to your computer. It's possible, that this file has a strange filename, like "STiYQuzTh", then you've to rename the file.
"STiYQuzTh" -> "STiYQuzTh.txt" or
"STiYQuzTh" -> "STiYQuzTh.html"
When you open the file and there are just strange letters in it, maybe your browser doesn't understand file-uris, in this case, you have to copy your text into a file on your computer.
