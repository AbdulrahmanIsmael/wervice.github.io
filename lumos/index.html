What is Lumos?

Lumos makes safe collaboration easy and keeps it productive.
You and your members can manage, share, and download all your important files from a central place.
Lumos runs on Windows and Linux computers.
It comes with several helpful features by default.
You can use the simple chat, the link sharing, the encryption, and more directly from your browser.


Safety & Protection


When hosting a cloud, security is very important.
Lumos makes it easy for you and your members to protect files and choose strong passwords.
Viruses could be a serious threat.
Lumos filters malicious files and prevents executables from being uploaded using ClamAV. To protect the file upload, Lumos uses HTTPS by default.

Collaboration & Communication


Communication works fast and stable using the chat feature.
You can share files and information quickly with your members without external servers.
To share files with people who don't have an account on your servers, you can create sharing links.
